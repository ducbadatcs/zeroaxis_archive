April 30, 1990

Robbery deemed scripted by court

On April 29, an attempted robbery was reported at Mr Martin Charlotter's home at 124 Marie St, Harile. However, the act had been decided by the local court as an act.
Mr Martin had been reportedly bribing his friends so that they would go in his house and mess things up, in order to look like he had been attacked / robbed. The reason behind this act is unknown. The verdict for this case, is Mr Martin has to pay $5000 to the court, and no further charges.
