13-September-1968

ALERT FOR ALL IVERIANS IN CITY OF HARILE, THERVOTA

The City of Harile police recently received numerous notices of Iverians missing or killed by an unknow force. While we are investigating the case, we advise Iverians to either leave the city, or if unable, please stay inside, lock your door, and avoid outside contact.

This warning is recorded on all radio stations in Harile, Thervota.

Alert sound, played several times.

Dear Iverian Community of Harile.
We are currenly facing an unknown threat towards Iverians in the city. Numerous Iverians were reported missing, murdered, and kidnapped in broad daylight. We therefore warn you to avoid outside contact, only go when it's necessary. We will update...

A loud buzzing sound then played, interrupting the narrator. A high pitched inhumane voice can be heard in the audio.

DO NOT LOOK UP
IT'S NOT WHERE YOU BELONG
