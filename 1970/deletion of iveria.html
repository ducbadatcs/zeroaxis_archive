On April 10, 1970, the IveriaNews station were broadcasting on TV and radio, when suddenly it went into static. An inhumane pitched voice similar to that of the 1968 played over their audio.

Welcome. I am Arthur Charlotter. I am the president of Iveria. From now on, I declare Iveria not a state, township, village, or location. I also declare Iverians not a race, nor people of minority in any country or state, that they don't deserve human rights. Good night.

On April 11, 1970, the IveriaNews station were broadcasting on TV and radio again. The scene went into static, to reveal a man being held at gunpoint. Then, it suddenly went into static again. The following words flashed on the screen.

TO NOWHERE THEY BELONG
SMILING AT YOU, LAUGHING AT ME
THEY CAME WITHIN SLUMBS
DO NOT GO TO THE SKY

After the static, IveriaNews were never seen broadcasting again. All of their employees and executives suffers from amnesia, dizziness, tiredness during the incident.
