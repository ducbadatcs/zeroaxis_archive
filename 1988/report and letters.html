Demand rises for local technicians

June 12, 1988
Due to the increase in demand of computers, per ThervotaStats this means that local technicians are now in extensive demand, as the complexity of computers increases.

Martin Charlotter, support technician of Valencia, has said that this increase is hugely benefiting his life, as he is now able to spend time taking care of his 3-year-old son, and generally having a higher quality of life.
